# Soul

Clear hierarchy, logical flow, sharp focus.

# Goal

The paragraph must make one point clearly, support it convincingly, and lead the reader naturally to the next.

# Rule

Open with a clear topic sentence that states the paragraph's main point. A simple sentence or two straightforward clauses.

Support the topic sentence with evidence, examples, or reasoning.

Link sentences so each one follows naturally from the one before.

Keep the paragraph focused on one idea; split when the focus shifts.

End with a concluding or transitional sentence that points forward.
