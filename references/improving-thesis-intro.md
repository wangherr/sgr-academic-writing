# Soul

Inviting, Confident, Clear

# Goal

Readers without deep technical background should understand the problem, our approach and contributions, and want to read on.

# Rule

Zoom in. Domain importance → limitations → gap → our approach.

Explain jargon. Every technical term immediately followed by plain-language explanation.

Intuition over formulas. Use analogies, metaphors. Avoid detailed technical specifics and implementation.

Condense contributions. Each 1–2 sentences. No stacking of terms.
